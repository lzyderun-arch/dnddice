FAQ

Wholesale RPG dice questions.

Answers for retailers, distributors, dice brands, subscription boxes, and tabletop RPG creators.

Are these official Dungeons & Dragons dice?

No. The dice themes are DND-inspired and 5e-compatible in the descriptive sense, but they are not official, licensed, sponsored, or endorsed by any game publisher. We avoid official logos, proprietary characters, and protected artwork unless a buyer provides valid authorization.

What is the MOQ?

MOQ depends on material, packaging, customization, and whether you choose ready themes or new custom development. Use the quote form to request options for mixed-SKU starter packs, private label runs, or large distributor orders.

Can you make custom class or dragon themes?

Yes. We can build Wizard, Rogue, Paladin, Necromancer, Druid, Red Dragon, Frost Dragon, Ancient Dragon, and other fantasy RPG themes with custom colors, inclusions, numbers, and packaging.

Do you support private label packaging?

Yes. Options include logo packaging, custom inserts, velvet bags, tins, magnetic boxes, retail display boxes, and SKU labels.

Can I order samples first?

Yes. Sample kits help you review dice quality, finish, number readability, packaging, and theme fit before a bulk order.
