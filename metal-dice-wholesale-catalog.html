Wholesale FAQ

Common buyer questions about metal dice catalog.

What is the MOQ for metal dice?

Metal dice MOQ depends on plating, enamel color, engraving, sculpted detail, weight, and packaging. Send SKU codes first so we can quote the practical starting quantity.

Can I request a sample kit before bulk order?

Sample kits help check metal weight, number readability, plating color, edge finish, and retail packaging fit before larger production.

Can you quote multiple SKUs from this catalog?

Yes. Send a SKU list such as CYJ-1, GTL-2, JS-1, plus estimated quantity, delivery country, packaging preference, and launch timeline. We will quote the selected products together.

Can packaging be customized?

Yes. Common B2B options include velvet bags, gift boxes, tins, display cartons, insert cards, barcode labels, private label packaging, and carton marks.
