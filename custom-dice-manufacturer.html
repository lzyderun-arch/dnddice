FAQ

Custom dice manufacturer questions.

Can you make DND-inspired dice without using official IP?

Yes. We can develop original fantasy RPG dice themes such as dragon, wizard, rogue, paladin, necromancer, elemental, treasure, rune, and dungeon relic concepts. DND-inspired and 5e-compatible are descriptive compatibility terms only.

Do you support private label packaging?

Yes. Options include branded boxes, inserts, tins, velvet bags, display boxes, SKU labels, and subscription-box-ready packing.

Can I start with samples before bulk production?

Yes. Sample review is recommended for color, finish, number readability, packaging fit, and shelf presentation before a wholesale order.
