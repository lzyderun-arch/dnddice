What should buyers know before sourcing wholesale resin dice?

Wholesale resin dice are best used for mid-range and premium RPG assortments where buyers need strong visual themes, flexible SKU choices, and better shelf presentation than basic acrylic dice.

For faster pricing, treat resin dice as a program: choose a material style, confirm SKU mix, request samples, define packaging, then quote by quantity and delivery country.

FAQ

Wholesale resin dice questions.

Are resin dice suitable for game stores?

Yes. Resin dice are a strong game store category because they support theme variety, good-better-best pricing, and mixed shelf assortments.

Can I request a mixed resin dice assortment?

Yes. Mixed assortments are useful for testing demand before committing to larger repeat orders.

Do resin dice support private label packaging?

Yes. Resin dice can be packed in bags, tins, boxes, display cartons, insert cards, or brand-labeled retail packaging.

Should I order samples first?

For new buyers, yes. Samples help check finish, number readability, weight, packaging quality, and retail presentation.
