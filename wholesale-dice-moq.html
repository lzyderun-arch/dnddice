MOQ Guide

Wholesale dice MOQ depends on material, SKU mix, packaging, and customization.

For B2B buyers, minimum order quantity is not one fixed number. A game store testing catalog SKUs, a dice brand ordering private label boxes, and a Kickstarter creator producing custom dice all need different MOQ planning.

Direct answer

What is the MOQ for wholesale RPG dice?

Wholesale RPG dice MOQ is determined by the product type, selected SKUs, packaging, private label requirements, and delivery plan. Existing catalog products can usually be quoted faster because product photos, SKU references, and MOQ planning fields are already available.

Fully custom dice, special D20 faces, custom molds, private label boxes, and complex specialty dice need higher planning quantities because they require additional production setup, artwork confirmation, and packaging coordination.

MOQ FAQ

Common questions about wholesale dice MOQ.

What is the MOQ for wholesale RPG dice?

MOQ depends on material, SKU mix, packaging, customization, and shipping plan. Existing catalog SKUs are usually easier to quote than fully custom dice.

Can a buyer start with a sample kit?

Yes. Sample kits help buyers review product finish, weight, number readability, packaging, and retail presentation before confirming a bulk order.

Why does MOQ change by material?

Resin, sharp edge, liquid core, metal, hollow, oversized D20, and rotating dice use different production processes, finishing steps, and packaging requirements.

What should I send for a fast quote?

Send catalog page, SKU list, quantity target, packaging format, private label needs, delivery country, and target launch date.
