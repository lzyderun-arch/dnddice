FAQ

Custom DND dice questions.

Can you put official DND logos or character names on dice?

No. We recommend original fantasy RPG designs and descriptive compatibility language only. This protects the buyer and keeps the product usable for broader tabletop RPG audiences.

Can custom dice include a brand logo?

Yes. Buyer-owned logos can be used on packaging, inserts, SKU labels, or a simple custom d20 face when the artwork is suitable for production.
