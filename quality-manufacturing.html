Quality and Manufacturing

Quality control for wholesale RPG dice programs.

B2B buyers need consistent finish, packaging accuracy, and repeatable replenishment. This page explains what we check before shipment.

Dice finish

Surface polish, sharp edge consistency, number readability, color alignment, and visible defect control.

Theme accuracy

Dragon, class, elemental, and dungeon relic series are checked against approved samples and packaging names.

Export packing

Carton labels, SKU separation, inner protection, display box packing, and photo records before shipment.

What B2B customers should confirm before production.

  • Approved sample photos and physical samples.
  • Acceptable variance for handmade resin colors and inclusions.
  • Packaging artwork, barcode labels, carton marks, and shipping terms.
  • Inspection standard for mixed-SKU wholesale shipments.
