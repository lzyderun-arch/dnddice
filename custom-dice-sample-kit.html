Direct answer

What is a custom RPG dice sample kit?

A custom RPG dice sample kit is a physical review pack for B2B buyers. It can include catalog dice SKUs, material references, theme samples, packaging examples, and private label packaging options.

For game stores, sample kits help test retail shelf appeal. For dice brands and distributors, sample kits help compare materials, finish quality, packaging fit, barcode labels, and brand presentation before placing a wholesale order.

Sample kit types

Choose the right sample kit for your buying stage.

Catalog SKU kit

Best for buyers selecting existing resin, metal, hollow, or specialty dice from catalog pages.

Material review kit

Best for comparing resin, sharp edge, liquid core, metal, hollow, oversized D20, or rotating dice formats.

Packaging review kit

Best for private label buyers choosing bags, tins, boxes, inserts, displays, labels, and carton marks.

Custom concept kit

Best for Kickstarter creators or brands planning custom colors, custom D20 faces, or exclusive product lines.

Sample Kit FAQ

Common questions about custom dice sample kits.

What is included in a custom dice sample kit?

A sample kit can include catalog dice, material samples, finish references, theme samples, packaging examples, and private label packaging options depending on the project.

Why request samples before bulk order?

Samples help buyers check finish, weight, balance, number readability, color accuracy, packaging fit, and retail presentation before approving MOQ and bulk production.

Can sample kits include private label packaging?

Yes. Buyers can review velvet bags, tins, gift boxes, display cartons, insert cards, barcode labels, and private label box structures.

What should I send before requesting a sample kit?

Send target product category, SKU list, buyer type, delivery country, packaging requirements, launch date, and whether the sample is for catalog sourcing or custom production.
