Wholesale Guide

How game stores choose wholesale RPG dice.

The best wholesale dice programs help retailers balance visual appeal, price point, replenishment, and shelf presentation.

Theme mix matters

A strong starter order usually combines dragon dice for premium buyers, class dice for 5e players, elemental dice for color-based choice, and dungeon relic dice for DM gifts. This gives store staff simple sales language and helps customers find a dice set quickly.

Packaging affects margin

Loose dice bags are efficient for entry-level sales, while tins, magnetic boxes, and display boxes create higher perceived value. Retailers should compare landed cost, display footprint, and expected sell-through before choosing a wholesale dice assortment.

Sample before bulk

Physical samples let a buyer check number readability, finish, weight, packaging, and customer fit before committing to a private label dice run.
