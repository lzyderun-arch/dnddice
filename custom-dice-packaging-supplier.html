Packaging as a product system

Good dice packaging is more than a box.

B2B buyers need packaging that supports retail shelf display, gift value, barcode labeling, shipment protection, and future replenishment. A custom dice packaging supplier should help define structure, artwork areas, insert cards, SKU logic, and carton packing before production.

FAQ

Custom dice packaging supplier questions.

Can packaging be quoted before dice samples are finalized?

Yes, but final packaging dimensions should be confirmed after dice size, count, insert style, and sample fit are approved.

Can you make packaging for only d20 dice?

Yes. D20 packaging can be simple sleeves, small tins, magnetic boxes, foam inserts, or premium collector boxes.

What files are useful for packaging development?

Logo files, brand colors, product names, barcode or SKU requirements, reference packaging photos, and target retail channel are useful.
