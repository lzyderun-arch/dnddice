Buyer positioning

Use liquid core dice as a premium retail upgrade.

Liquid core dice are better suited for premium launches than price-entry assortments. They work well for gift sets, collector releases, campaign accessories, seasonal drops, and subscription box upgrades where buyers can see motion and detail.

  • Strong visual movement for short video content, product photos, and retail display.
  • Good match for elemental, arcane, dragon eye, treasure, celestial, and necromancer themes.
  • Private label boxes and inserts help explain the concept and justify premium positioning.
  • Sample approval is recommended to check clarity, motion, number contrast, and packaging fit.

FAQ

Liquid core wholesale questions.

Are liquid core dice good for game stores?

Yes, especially as a premium shelf item, gift set, or limited release. They should usually be sold alongside simpler wholesale dice to cover different price points.

Can liquid core dice be combined with custom packaging?

Yes. Boxes, tins, display packaging, and inserts are recommended because liquid core dice are often positioned as premium products.

Should I order samples first?

Yes. Samples help verify the movement effect, clarity, number readability, and packaging fit before bulk production.
