Wholesale FAQ

Common buyer questions about resin dice catalog.

What is the MOQ for resin dice?

Most listed resin dice SKUs show MOQ 100 for wholesale planning. Final MOQ can change by color finish, oversized D20 size, custom face, and packaging format.

Can I request a sample kit before bulk order?

Sample kits are recommended for resin finishes such as quicksand, eyeball, skull, decal, and oversized D20 dice before bulk production.

Can you quote multiple SKUs from this catalog?

Yes. Send a SKU list such as CDQM-1, DQM-3, PZ-2, plus estimated quantity, delivery country, packaging preference, and launch timeline. We will quote the selected products together.

Can packaging be customized?

Yes. Common B2B options include velvet bags, gift boxes, tins, display cartons, insert cards, barcode labels, private label packaging, and carton marks.
