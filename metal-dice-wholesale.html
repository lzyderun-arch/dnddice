Direct answer

Why add metal dice to a wholesale RPG dice assortment?

Metal dice give game stores and dice brands a higher-value product tier than standard resin or acrylic dice. They work well for premium shelves, gift sets, convention booths, collector drops, and private label product lines.

Because weight, finish, and packaging affect buyer satisfaction, metal dice should usually be sourced with physical sample approval before large orders.

Are metal dice good for wholesale game stores?

Yes. They work best as premium shelf products and gift sets, especially when paired with strong packaging and clear theme names.

Can I mix metal dice themes in one order?

Yes. Mixed metal assortments are common for buyer testing, game store launch orders, and distributor product reviews.

Can metal dice use custom packaging?

Yes. Because metal dice are heavier and more premium, custom packaging is recommended for protection and retail presentation.
