Wholesale FAQ

Common buyer questions about exotic dice catalog.

What is the MOQ for rotating and specialty dice?

Rotating, carousel, revolver, and specialty dice need MOQ confirmation by structure and assembly process. Send the SKU list and expected order size for an accurate quote.

Can I request a sample kit before bulk order?

Sample kits are strongly recommended for specialty formats so buyers can check rotation, assembly quality, gift-box fit, and retail presentation.

Can you quote multiple SKUs from this catalog?

Yes. Send a SKU list such as LP-1, D20-3, ZDJ-2, plus estimated quantity, delivery country, packaging preference, and launch timeline. We will quote the selected products together.

Can packaging be customized?

Yes. Common B2B options include velvet bags, gift boxes, tins, display cartons, insert cards, barcode labels, private label packaging, and carton marks.
