Custom workflow

From DND-inspired concept to wholesale-ready dice set.

  1. Brief and target market

    Share buyer type, country, theme direction, target retail price, expected order quantity, packaging requirement, and launch timeline.

  2. Theme and material proposal

    We map the concept into practical production options: resin, sharp edge, liquid core, metal-style finish, oversized d20, number color, and packaging tier.

  3. Artwork and packaging plan

    Confirm logo placement, custom d20 face, number fill, insert card, box structure, display box layout, SKU naming, and carton organization.

  4. Sample production

    Physical samples verify color, finish, readability, feel, packaging fit, and shelf presentation before bulk commitment.

  5. Pre-production approval

    Approved sample photos, packaging files, quantity breakdown, defect tolerance, shipping method, and delivery schedule are confirmed before production.

  6. Bulk production and QC

    Production follows approved samples with checks for surface polish, number fill, edge quality, packaging accuracy, and mixed-SKU packing.

  7. Export packing and reorder support

    Orders can be packed by SKU, theme, display box, or customer carton mark to support distributors, game stores, and subscription boxes.
