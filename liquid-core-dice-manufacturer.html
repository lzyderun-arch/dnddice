What does a liquid core dice manufacturer need to control?

A liquid core dice project needs control over the moving core effect, outer resin clarity, number contrast, weight feel, packaging fit, and sample approval. These dice are premium products, so the manufacturing process should be planned around buyer expectations and retail presentation.

For B2B buyers, the right workflow is sample-first: approve the motion effect and readability before confirming a larger MOQ.

FAQ

Liquid core manufacturing questions.

Are liquid core dice better than standard resin dice?

They are not always better; they are positioned differently. Liquid core dice are best when the buyer wants a premium visual product, while standard resin dice work better for broader assortment volume.

Can liquid core dice use DND-inspired themes?

Yes, original 5e-compatible fantasy themes such as frost mage, dragon eye, storm core, arcane orb, celestial, and shadow relic can be developed without using official game logos or protected artwork.
