Direct answer

What packaging options are available for custom RPG dice?

Common custom RPG dice packaging options include velvet bags, zip bags, tins, magnetic gift boxes, paper boxes, display cartons, insert cards, barcode labels, header cards, and private label box designs.

For wholesale buyers, packaging should be selected together with MOQ, SKU mix, retail price point, and shipping plan. A simple bag is usually easier for starter orders, while a fully printed box or display carton is better for private label product lines.

Packaging FAQ

Common questions about custom dice packaging.

Can dice packaging be private labeled?

Yes. Private label dice packaging can include brand logos, custom artwork, insert cards, barcode labels, carton marks, and retail display formats.

Does packaging affect MOQ and price?

Yes. Packaging affects MOQ, unit cost, production lead time, carton size, shipping weight, and retail presentation.

Can I review packaging before bulk order?

Yes. Packaging samples can be included in a sample kit so buyers can check material, print quality, structure, and dice fit.

What files are needed for packaging?

Send logo files, artwork references, label text, barcode requirements, SKU list, target quantity, and delivery country.
