Quote checklist

What to include for a faster quote.

  • Target dice themes: dragon, class, elemental, dungeon relic, d20, or custom concept.
  • Material preference: resin, sharp edge, liquid core, metal, oversized d20, or mixed.
  • Estimated MOQ or first order size: sample kit, 300 sets, 1,000 sets, mixed SKU order, or private label run.
  • Packaging format: velvet bag, box, tin, display, gift set, subscription box, or private label packaging.
  • Branding needs: custom d20 face, logo insert, SKU label, barcode, display header, or carton mark.
  • Delivery country, target launch date, and expected reorder plan.

MOQ and sample note

There is no single MOQ for every project. Stock-style mixed assortments, sample kits, custom colorways, custom faces, and private label packaging each have different starting quantities. Send your target quantity and we will recommend the practical route.
