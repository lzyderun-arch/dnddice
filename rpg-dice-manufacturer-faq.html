Manufacturer FAQ

Questions wholesale buyers ask before choosing a dice supplier.

What does an RPG dice manufacturer supply?

An RPG dice manufacturer can supply polyhedral dice sets, resin dice, sharp edge dice, liquid core dice, metal dice, hollow dice, specialty dice, sample kits, and private label packaging.

Can a manufacturer support private label packaging?

Yes. Private label support can include logo packaging, insert cards, barcode labels, display boxes, carton marks, and retail-ready pack formats.

How should buyers compare manufacturers?

Compare product range, sample quality, MOQ flexibility, packaging capability, communication speed, QC process, catalog depth, and repeat-order support.

What is needed for a quote?

Send product category, SKU list or custom concept, estimated quantity, packaging needs, delivery country, launch date, and buyer type.

Can game stores use catalog SKUs?

Yes. Catalog SKUs are a practical route for game stores that need faster assortment planning and lower-risk first orders.

Can Kickstarter creators request custom dice?

Yes. Kickstarter creators should send campaign timeline, target quantity, theme concept, packaging plan, and sample requirements.
