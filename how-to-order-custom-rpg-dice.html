Direct answer

What is the easiest way to order custom RPG dice?

The easiest way is to begin with a SKU list or a clear custom concept. For catalog products, send page links and SKU codes. For custom dice, send material preference, colors, artwork, logo requirements, packaging format, target quantity, delivery country, and launch date.

For first orders, sample kits reduce risk because buyers can review finish, readability, dice feel, and packaging before confirming MOQ and bulk production.

Order FAQ

Common questions about ordering custom RPG dice.

Can I order catalog SKUs instead of fully custom dice?

Yes. Catalog SKUs are usually faster to quote and are a practical route for first wholesale orders.

Should I order samples first?

Yes. Samples help verify product quality, readability, finish, and packaging before approving bulk production.

What affects production lead time?

Material, custom artwork, mold setup, packaging, quantity, QC requirements, and shipping method all affect lead time.

What should I send for a quote?

Send SKU list or concept, material, quantity, packaging needs, logo files, delivery country, buyer type, and launch date.
