Production stages

Recommended dice manufacturing steps for crowdfunding.

  1. Pre-launch quote

    Estimate dice type, material, quantity tiers, packaging, sample schedule, and shipping assumptions before campaign pricing.

  2. Sample and photography

    Use approved samples for campaign images, reward graphics, and realistic backer expectations.

  3. Backer quantity confirmation

    After funding, confirm final quantities by SKU, add-on, stretch goal, and packaging type.

  4. Bulk production and QC

    Produce to approved samples with checks on color, number fill, surface finish, packaging, and mixed packing.

  5. Export packing

    Pack by SKU, carton mark, pledge type, or fulfillment partner requirement for smoother handoff.

FAQ

Kickstarter dice manufacturing questions.

Can you quote before my campaign launches?

Yes. Pre-launch quoting helps you estimate reward pricing, stretch goal thresholds, packaging costs, and shipping assumptions.

Can dice be made for an original RPG world?

Yes. Original campaign symbols, colors, factions, schools of magic, monsters, locations, and relic concepts can become custom dice themes.

Can you ship directly to backers?

This site is positioned for B2B production and bulk delivery. Direct-to-backer fulfillment should be discussed separately with your fulfillment plan and destination mix.
